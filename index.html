Rules of the Game!

This is a re-imagining of the classic game of 'Simon Says'. If you're an 80's (or even 90's kid) you may remember some version of this game. Essentially when you start, the window to the right will begin playing a sequence of buttons and audio, it is your job to remember their order and repeat them - try and see how many points you can accumulate!
